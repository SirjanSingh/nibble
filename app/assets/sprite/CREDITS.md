# Creature sprite

v0.1 ships a **self-contained CSS/SVG blob creature** (see
`app/windows/creature.css`) — no external asset, no attribution, works
offline. Expression states: idle, sleeping, happy, alert, shocked,
reconnecting.

## Optional: drop in a CC0 spritesheet

To replace the CSS blob with a pixel-art creature, add a spritesheet here as
`creature.png` and switch `.sprite` in `creature.css` to use
`background-image`. Vetted CC0 sources (no attribution, commercial OK):

- Kenney "Tiny Creatures" — https://kenney.nl/assets/tiny-creatures — CC0 1.0
- Kenney "Monster Builder Pack" — https://kenney.nl/assets/monster-builder-pack — CC0 1.0
- OpenGameArt "Animated Slime" — https://opengameart.org/content/animated-slime — CC0
- OpenGameArt "Tiny Creatures" — https://opengameart.org/content/tiny-creatures — CC0 1.0

Verify the per-asset license on the download page (itch.io licenses are
author-set and vary).
